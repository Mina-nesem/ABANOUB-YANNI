Woh Am i ?

Abanoub Yanni.. holds a Bachelor of Music Education with distinction.. He started learning the piano since the age of 10, teaching music at the age of 16 to play the piano, and he added to it the teaching of eastern and western singing from his academic studies with Professor Gabriela Bouda and the academician at the College of Music Education .. He studied music composition at the Music Conservatory. Interested in studying psychology, psychotherapy, and activities within the Mental Health Hospital in the colors of psychological therapy with art.
